Welcome to The Grand Haven Hotel

Featuring complimentary Wi-Fi and an exquisite dining experience, The Grand Haven Hotel offers luxurious accommodations in the heart of the city. Guests can unwind in our year-round outdoor pool or stay active in the fully-equipped fitness center. Enjoy a selection of fine beverages at our stylish bar, with free private parking available on-site for your convenience. Our 24-hour front desk is always ready to assist with any of your needs. The vibrant City Central Park is just 2 miles from The Grand Haven Hotel, and the iconic Grand Theater is 1.8 miles away. The International Airport is a short 10-mile drive, making your travel easy and hassle-free.

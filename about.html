About us

The Grand Haven Hotel

Established in 1925, The Grand Haven Hotel has a rich history of excellence and hospitality. Originally built as a luxury retreat for travelers seeking relaxation by the coast, the hotel has evolved over the decades while preserving its timeless charm. Through the years, The Grand Haven has hosted dignitaries, celebrities, and discerning guests from around the world, making it a symbol of elegance and sophistication. Carefully restored to maintain its architectural grandeur, the hotel blends historic beauty with modern conveniences, offering a unique experience that reflects its storied past and enduring legacy.
